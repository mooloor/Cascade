Child Soldier

Requirements:

  • None

Effects:

  • Basic familiarity with a weapon class of your choice
  • +1 CON
  • Social Standing = 0
  • Education = 0
  • Your character immediately ages to 16

Description:

  • Some time after you could walk, someone handed you a weapon and told you you were going to fight for them. Child soldiers are normally a desperate final measure, so the war you were pulled into was likely close-fought and brutal.
