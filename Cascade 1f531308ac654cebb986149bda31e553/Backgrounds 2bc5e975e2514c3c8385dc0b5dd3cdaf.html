Backgrounds

Created@Apr 21, 2020 12:19 AM
TagsNot DonePlayerRules

Once your character leaves childhood (reaches 16 years of age or the equivalent), you may start taking backgrounds. These simulate how your character spends their life, jobs they have, experience and skills they gain, and connections they make in various elements of society.

You may only take a background if you meet its requirements or if you previously took that background.

Reputation:

There are 5 reputation stats, or reps: Military, Government, Wealth, Academia, and Crime. These represent your experience, influence, or contacts within a certain group, and some jobs/backgrounds require you to have certain relations with a group before you are able to take it.

Aging:

As your character lives their life, they age. Maturity is achieved at 25 years old, from then on, after every background taken, you must make a check for every single stat (except for LCK). The difficulty of this check is equal to your age minus 25. If you fail the check, you lose 1 level in that stat.

Anti-aging treatments: The difficulty of the aging check can be reduced by 1 by paying an amount of credits equal to the difference between your character's age and 20 x300. This reduction is permanent. This can be purchased as often as you like.

Cost of living: Additionally, as your character lives their life, they incur various payments, such as rent, food, medical bills, etc. This cost is equal to your character's social standing x1000 per year. If your last background had a duration of more than one year, you must pay the cost times the duration of the background. If you cannot pay the requisite amount, your social standing decreases until you can. If you so choose, you may spend five thousand credits to increase your social standing by 1 (up to 10)
