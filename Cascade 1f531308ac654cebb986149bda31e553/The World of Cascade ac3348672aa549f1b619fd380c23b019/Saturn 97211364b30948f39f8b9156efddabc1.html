Saturn

The "official" government of saturn, the most well-known one that most of the other governments recognise, is the Conflux Station (name subject to change)

This is an absolutely massive ring-style habitat, filled with every sort of opulence you can imagine

It acts as a combination government (inner ring) and rich people leisure area (outer ring)

There are docks for other standard cylinder habs as well, for the wealthy to get on or off the station

Obviously, you can't just have a government of rich people, they need a lot of infrastructure to support them, which they certainly aren't going to pay for with their own money

And they sure as hell aren't gonna be working

So there are basically 4 castes in the saturn government

At the top, you have the rich, who generally live in their own cylinder habs around the planet

Below them, you have the enforcers. They are clad in all black, with opaque face shields, and armed with shock batons

They are given relatively free reign in terms of who they prosecute and for what, and have permission to surveil anyone in or below their position

They are paid decently and are generally given good facilities and some benefits

Below them, you have the engineers

These are usually contractors, flown in from outside

They maintain the systems in the Conflux, along with the automated farms, life support, and generators keeping everyone alive.

However, they are generally not allowed in the open areas of the Conflux, as access there is limited by personal wealth.

Most of the station is reachable by various crawlspaces and maintenance corridors, kept out of sight of the wealthy

Finally, below them, you have the Servants and the Miners

The Servants are a clone caste, used wherever human intervention is necessary (or desired) and will be in view of the Wealthy

There are 2 varieties of Servant, male and female

They all look the same, to more easily allow the Wealthy to not see them. They are an invisible caste, designed to be unseen and unheard, obedient, and not particularly bright

This is not to say, however, that the cloning facilities on the Conflux are only capable of producing 2 types of Servants

No, they can create whatever you desire, for whatever purpose you may have

Finally, you have the miners

They are the desperate, the lost, the failed, and, occasionally, the children of the Servants or the Engineers

They operate the endless quantities of probes that extract He3 from the upper atmosphere of the gas giant

These probes have a relatively shallow orbit, dangling collectors deep into the atmosphere, collecting the gases to be processed and sold to the rest of the system

The Miners are kept isolated, in communities no larger than extended families, and not in good conditions

While not quite 1920s tenement levels, it's not too far off. They're basically medieval peasants, barely paid subsistence wages

At all levels, bar the Wealthy, the Shadows operate

These are a branch of the Enforcers, basically acting like the Stasi of the GDR

They encourage a culture of spying, recruit informants, and collate data

All individuals are encouraged to inform on their neighbors in exchange for 1 month's wages in the case that the information pans out

Surprisingly, this has never been broken and is actually processed in a fairly timely manner

No matter what level you are, if you give the Shadows actionable information, you will receive an additional month's wages rapidly upon that information being verified
