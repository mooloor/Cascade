  • Attachments
    • Charge Module

      Armor Attachment

      Effects:

      Must be equipped on power armor

      Allows the user to charge 10m in a straight line at a cost of 5MP and 5CP.

      Anything in front of you will be carried with you.

      If you hit a wall, the charge ends. Any enemies caught between you and the wall take a major wound of damage to all body parts.

    • Quick-loading Stock

      Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      Incompatible with other Stock attachments

    • Recoil-Dampening Stock

      Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      Steadying removes an additional stack of kickback

    • Infrared Optics

      Armor/Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      Allows you to detect heat sources

      Eliminates disadvantage for firing through smoke or in darkness

    • Integrated AutoDoc

      Armor/Limb Attachment

      Effects:

      Whenever you would take bleeding or cascading damage, this attachment eliminates it for this limb, as long as the attachment is charged.

      Can hold up to 5 charges

      1 charge stops 1 instance of bleeding or cascading damage

      Can be recharged with any medical mechanite supply

    • Bayonet

      Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      You have a knife attached to the front of your gun. By default, this knife has the stats of a Titanium Sword, but it can be swapped out at will.

      The knife may be removed for 5MP, at which point it functions like a normal melee weapon

    • Night Vision Optics

      Armor/Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      Negates disadvantage on any attacks made from darkness, gain a stack of disadvantage if used in normal light.

      Can be deactivated at will

    • Flashlight

      Armor/Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      Projects a cone of bright light out to 10m, and dim light another 10m beyond that

      Enemies which come within 2m are blinded, and suffer a stack of disadvantage on any attacks

    • Angled Grip

      Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      +1 stack of kickback removed

    • Scope

      Weapon Attachment

      Effects:

      +1 to hit

    • Weapon Stalk

      Armor Attachment

      Effects:

      Pick a ranged weapon. This weapon is attached to your armor by a gimballed, double-jointed arm. You may make attacks with this weapon for 5CP. This weapon does not benefit from bonuses to hit and cannot aim, but does not suffer from recoil and can fire in any direction.

    • Thrusterpack

      Armor/Limb Attachment

      Effects:

      You gain the ability to jump up to 5m in the air (stacks up to 25m with multiple thrusterpacks) at a cost of 7MP. You take no fall damage from these jumps.

      Must be attached to the torso or symmetrically on limbs.

    • Jetpack

      Armor Attachment

      Effects:

      You may hover up to 10m in the air for up to 30s and move at double MP cost. As long as you have not expended all of your fuel, you may choose to not take fall damage, at the cost of 1s of fuel. At the end of the duration, if you are still in the air, you quickly but gently fall up to 10m, negating any fall damage. If you fall further than this without fuel, you take fall damage as normal.

      Must be attached to the torso.

    • Polyceramic Plating

      Armor/Limb Attachment

      Effects:

      +1 armor

      -5 MP

    • Mantis Blade

      Armor Attachment

      Effects:

      You have a 3-jointed blade attached to your armor at a point of your choice. This blade has the stats of a Titanium Sword by default, but can be upgraded.
