Stats

Strength - Determines how brawny you are

Constitution - Determines how hardy you are

Agility - Determines how fast you are

Dexterity - Determines how fast your reflexes are

Perception - Determines how observant you are

Charisma - Determines how suave you are

Intelligence - Determines how well you learn and how much you know

Abilities:

Combat Training

Requirements: None

Gain +5 CP.

This skill may be taken more than once.

Endurance Training

Requirements: None

Gain +5 MP.

This skill may be taken more than once.

Specialist Training

Requirements: None

Gain +3 AP.

This skill may be taken more than once.

Pistol Training

Requirements: None

You can use pistols without any downsides

Rifle Training

Requirements: None

You can use rifles without any downsides

Melee Training

Requirements: None

You can use melee weapons without any downsides

Special Training

Requirements: None

You can use special weapons without any downsides

[!]Handful of Pain

Requirements: 5 AGL && 5 DEX

You may throw 2 items at once at the same target for the same CP as a normal throwing attack.

+3 CP

[!]Mobile Grappler

Requirements: 6 STR

While restraining someone, you may move with them at double MP cost.

Improved Disarm

Requirements: 5 STR

On a successful grapple, you may chose to disarm your target instead of restraining them. If you do so, their weapon is thrown 1d4 metres away, in a direction of your choice.

Disarm Mastery

Requirements: 6 STR && Improved Disarm

When you successfully disarm someone, you may choose to use their weapon instead of throwing it away, provided you are capable of wielding it.

[!]Snip-snap!

Requirements: 7 STR

While restraining an target, you may attempt a melee attack with a stack of advantage at a cost of 10CP. If successful, they take no damage, and you may cripple a restrained target's limb (except for the head or torso).

[!]Black-bag

Requirements: 6 DEX

While in stealth, you may instantly restrain a target within grappling range at normal CP cost.

Nothing but Dreams...

Requirements: 7 DEX && 7 STR && Black-bag && Snip-snap!

While restraining a target, you may attempt a melee attack on the target's head. If successful, they take no damage, but their head is crippled.

Controlling Grapple

Requirements: Disarm Mastery, Mobile Grappler

While restraining a target, they always take their turn after you in the initiative order. When they are released, they return to their previous place in the initiative order. Additionally, when you successfully restrain a target, you may knock them prone instead of restraining them.

[!]Try Again

Requirements: AGL 3 && DEX 3

You may parry counterattacks.

Off-hand Training

Requirements: AGL 3

You may make unarmed attacks while wielding a melee weapon in your other hand.

Sucker Punch

Requirements: Off-hand Training

If an opponent successfully blocks one of your melee attacks, you may immediately follow up with an unarmed attack of 50% CP cost.

Killing Blow

Requirements: DEX 7

Attempt a melee attack at triple CP cost. If it succeeds, the attack ignores all armor.

Coup de Grace

Requirements: Killing Blow

Spend all of your AP, along with your remaining MP and CP to make an attack that deals 2 lethal wounds to a target. You must have at least 5 MP and CP to use this attack.

Good Try

Requirements: Take-back, Try Again, STR 6

When you successfully parry an opponent, instead of counterattacking, you may attempt to knck the enemy prone. If you do so, make an unarmed attack roll, the target must succeed in an AGL check against that to avoid being knocked prone.

[!]Bladecatcher

Requirements: None

You may attempt to parry melee attacks while unarmed.

Aggressive Struggling

Requirements: None

You may attempt to make unarmed attacks while restrained with a stack of disadvantage.

Cheap Shot

Requirements: Aggressive struggling

Unarmed attacks against prone or restrained targets gain a stack of advantage.

Unarmed Combat Training

Requirements: Aggressive Struggling && STR 5

Your fists deal a significant wound of damage instead of a minor one.

Cheaper Shot

Requirements: Cheap Shot && Good Try

On critical hits, your target is knocked prone.

-1 critical hit threshold.

Thousand Hand Slap

Requirements: Unarmed Combat Training && DEX 7

When you make a successful unarmed attack, your next unarmed attack costs half as much CP (minimum of 1). This resets on a miss.

[!]Good Positioning

Requirements: Unarmed Combat Training

Unarmed counterattacks ignore armor.

Iron Will

Requirements: CON 5

You may spend 5CP to brace, reducing all damage by one significant wound until the start of your next turn.

Gritted Teeth

Requirements: Iron Will

If you have a crippled limb, you may take a minor wound to the limb and your torso to perform an action which requires that limb. This damage cannot be blocked.

Blood Rage

Requirements: CON 5

Gain +5 AP while damaged. Eliminate negative AP/MP/CP penalty while damaged.

Red Haze

Requirements: Blood Rage

Gain +1 MP or +1 CP for your next turn whenever you are hit during an enemy turn.

Light Feet

Requirements: DEX 4 && AGL 4

You may choose to move at 2x AP cost. If you do so, enemies suffer -5 to spotting you.

Steady Breath

Requirements: DEX 6

Your first aim action taken each turn while in stealth costs no CP.

Deadly Aim

Requirements: Steady Breath

Wounds from stealth attacks are upgraded by 1 level.

Blinding Speed

Requirements: Light Feet && 6 AGL

As a reaction, if you are spotted, you may immediately take a bonus turn with any remaining AP you have. If you break line of sight during this bonus turn, you are not spotted. If you kill the enemy or enemies who spotted you during this bonus turn, you are not spotted.

Rapid Repositioning

Requirements: AGL 7

At the end of your turn, if you are not in stealth, you gain 10MP to use immediately. This bonus MP lasts until the end of your turn.

Preternatural Balance

Requirements: AGL 5

Gain a stack of advantage when attempting to resist forced state-changes (i.e. being knocked prone).

High-Strung

Requirements: AGL 5

Your first dodge each turn costs no MP.

Quick Reflexes

Requirements: AGL 5 && DEX 5

Counterattacks cost no CP.

Evasion Training

Rquirements: AGL 5 && DEX 5 && High-Strung

Dodging costs 1 less MP.

6th Sense

Requirements: AGL 7 && Quick Reflexes

You may parry attacks made behind you.

Quicker Reflexes

Rquirements: DEX 6 && 6th Sense && Quick Reflexes

You may attempt to catch any projectile flying your way.

Parry Training

Requirements: Quick Reflexes && Try Again && DEX 6

Your first parry each turn costs no CP.

[!]Grumble grumble

Requirements: CHR 5

You can effect an extremely intimidating voice.

Knock...knock

Requirements: CHR 5

You may menacingly perform any action.

[!]Assisted Initimidation [Me and my friends here...]

Requirements: CHR 5

When attempting to intimidate someone, any attempt made is also made by anyone else in your group.

[!]Tricks of the Trade

Requirements CHR 7

You cannot be intimidated.

Looks that kill

Requirements: CHR 7

If someone attempts to start a conversation, you may make a nonverbal intimidation check to prevent the conversation from happening.

Show of Force

Requirements: CHR 8

If you kill someone in a non-combat scenario and there is no law enforcement nearby, it counts as successfully intimidating everyone in the vicinity.

[!]Enraging Conversation [Call my bluff]

Requirements: Looks that kill && Show of Force

If you attempt to intimidate someone, they must either succumb to the attempt or instantly initiate combat.

But, wait! [Just-let-me-say-this!]

Requirements: CHR 4

The first time a conversation partner ends a conversation, you may instead act as if it was still going.

[!]The Invisibles

Requirements: CHR 4

You can vanish into a crowd.

Disguise Training

Requirements: CHR 5

You can obscure your appearance, rendering you unable to be identified by sight alone.

Imitation Training

Requirements: CHR 5

You can imitate the voice of anyone you have heard speak for 10 minutes or more or been in conversation with

Smooth Talker

Requirements: CHR 5

Any stammers, stutters, etc you make while speaking in-character are treated as though they didn't happen.

Imitation Perfection

Requirements: CHR 6 && Imitation Training

You can imitate any handwriting you’ve seen, provided you have seen a reasonable sample.

[!]Forger

Requirements: CHR 7 && Imitation Perfection

You may forge items. These forgeries pass common muster, but skilled craftspeople or those knowledgeable in the requisite field can identify them.

Quick Thinker

Requirements: CHR 6 && Smooth Talker

The first time you fail an attempt at deception, you may continue the conversation as if you hadn’t.

Control the Conversation

Requirements: CHR 8 && Quick Thinker

A conversation does not end until you let it end or combat is initiated.

Inspiring Nature

Requirements: CHR 6

You may spend 15 AP and move an ally to the top of the initiative order

[!]Backdoors

Requirements: INT 6

When you stop hacking a system, you may leave a backdoor, allowing you to begin hacking it again from the point you finished.

Feinting

Requirements: AGL 6

When an enemy dodges your first melee attack against them, you may make a counterattack against them.

Sharp-eyed

Requirements: None

Gain a stack of advantage on spotting characters who are in stealth.

Paranoid

Requirements: None

Gain +3 to initiative rolls. You cannot be surprised while conscious. Enemies gain no advantage stacks when attempting to attack you when hidden.

Bull Rush

Requirements: None

If you spend all of your MP in one turn, gain +5 CP until the start of your next turn.

Sniping Specialist

Requirements: DEX 5

Reduce your falloff modifier by 1/3 metres.

Close Combat Specialist

Requirements: AGL 5

Reduce your proximity modifier by 1/2 metres.

+2 AP

Counterattack Training

Requirements: AGL 5

If you successfully parry an attack, you may make a free unarmed counterattack. If this attack hits, your opponent loses 3 AP until the end of their next turn.

Berserker Training

Requirements: STR 4 && DEX 4 && AGL 4

While wielding a melee weapon in either hand, you have 2 attempts to parry any attack (that you would be able to parry normally). Additionally, when you make a melee attack, you may follow up with another melee attack at -2 CP cost.

+3 CP

Kata Training

Requirements: DEX 6

While wielding a ranged weapon in both hands, if you move after making a ranged attack, your next ranged attack costs half as much CP.

+3 MP

Stick and Move

Requirements: AGL 5 && PER 5

While wielding a single ranged weapon, after moving, your first aim action has -1 CP cost.

+3 MP

Stable Shooting

Requirements: PER 6

While crouching, you have +1 to hit with ranged attacks. If you are prone, this bonus increases to +2.

Tough

Requirements: CON 6

Bleeding and cascading damage are reduced by a level

Eidetic Memory

Requirements: None

You have perfect recall of anything you've experienced in your life.

[!]Zero-G Maneuvering

Requirements: AGL 4

You can move at will in Zero-G, at triple MP cost.

Proficient

Requirements: None

Gain an additional point with which to buy dice for any one stat. This does not increase that stat, but you may reallocate all of your dice points

[!]Last Wind

Requirements: None

You may spend any dice to add their max value to any role. You may do this after you know the results of the roll. This removes the dice from your pool for that stat for 24 hours.

Sentinel

Requirements: PER 6

When an enemy makes a reaction, you may makes a reaction against them first.

Clothesline

Requirements: None

When you make a melee reaction attack against an enemy, they lose all of their MP until the start of their next turn.

War Cry

Requirements: None

You may spend 10CP to unleash a fearsome battle cry. All enemies in the vicinity must succeed in a CHR check against you or become frightened.

Nimble

Requirements: AGL 5

Gain a stack of advantage when attempting to resist grappling effects.

+3 MP

Jump Attack

Requirements: DEX 5 && AGL 5

If you perform a melee attack immediately after jumping in the direction of an enemy, your attack damage is upgraded by one level

+2 AP

Mechanic

Requirements: INT 6

Gain a stack of advantage when attempting to repair something

Wait for your moment

Requirements: AGL 4

You may move your turn up to 3 steps down the initiative order. Doing this ends your turn until it comes up again.
