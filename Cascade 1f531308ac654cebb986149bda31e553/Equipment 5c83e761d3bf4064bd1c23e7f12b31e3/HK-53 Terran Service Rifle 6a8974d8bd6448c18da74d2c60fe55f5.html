Description:

Originally designed by Heckler and Koch for the European Federation United Military, some variant of this weapon has been the first choice of most Earth-based militaries since its introduction in 2453. This weapon propels caseless cartridges using electrically-ignited solid fuel. Widespread, simple and reliable, this rifle has been known to last a lifetime under proper care.
